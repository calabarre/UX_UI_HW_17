an introduction to myself (..and a half breakup letter to my architecture degree)

I’m an Australian multi-disciplinary designer focusing on Architecture, UI and UX design. Based in Sydney, Australia.

Over the past 3 years I have been designing buildings and infrastructures made to stand the test of time which was fulled by my love to create spacial experiences for people. This is how I discovered the UX realm of designing for people and experience design.

A Third Culture Individual who grew up in Asia with its diverse culture and experiences, this outlook helped shape the designer, student and person I am today.
